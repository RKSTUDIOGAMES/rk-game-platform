🔐 Privacy Policy

Welcome to RK Games. Your privacy is extremely important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

  • Name and basic profile information
  • Email address for account creation and login
  • Player ID (YouTube handle)
  • Usage data such as activity, points, and interactions
  • Device-related information (for security and login tracking)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To provide game features and services
  • To send OTP for login verification
  • To track points, rewards, and leaderboard ranking
  • To improve platform performance and user experience

3. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Keep you logged in (remember device feature)
  • Improve security and prevent fraud
  • Analyze user behavior
  • Deliver relevant advertisements

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services such as:

  • Ad networks (e.g., Google AdSense)
  • Email services (e.g., SendGrid)
  • Analytics tools

These services may collect and process your data according to their own privacy policies.

5. Advertisements

RK Games displays advertisements to support the platform. These ads may:

  • Use cookies to show personalized ads
  • Track user engagement
  • Provide rewards based on ad viewing

Users must watch ads properly to earn rewards. Any attempt to bypass or manipulate ads is strictly prohibited.

6. Data Security

We implement strong security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Password encryption
  • OTP-based verification
  • Secure session management

However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. User Responsibilities

  • Do not share your login credentials
  • Use the platform honestly
  • Avoid multiple account creation
  • Report any suspicious activity

8. Data Retention

We retain user data as long as the account is active or as needed to provide services. Some data may be stored for security, legal, or analytical purposes.

9. Account Deletion

Users may request account deletion. Upon deletion:

  • Your personal data will be removed
  • Points and rewards will be lost
  • Some logs may remain for security purposes

10. Children's Privacy

RK Games is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

11. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Continued use of the platform means you accept the updated policy.

12. Your Consent

By using RK Games, you agree to this Privacy Policy and our Terms & Conditions.
